Our Profile

Harapan Bangsa High School, a distinguished educational institution under the aegis of the esteemed Nusa Bangsa Foundation, stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Indonesia. Since its inception in the year 2004, the school has held a steadfast commitment to nurturing and preparing the forthcoming generation to be architects of a brighter Indonesia.

Vision:

Our vision is to prepare and motivate our students for a rapidly changing world by instilling in them critical thinking skills, a global perspective, and a respect for core values of honesty, loyalty, perseverance, and compassion. Students will have success for today and be prepared for tomorrow.

Mission:

1. Nurturing Critical Thinkers: The school's foremost mission is to foster the growth of critical thinking skills among its students. Through an innovative and engaging pedagogical approach, Harapan Bangsa High School ensures that students are provided with opportunities to delve deeply into subjects, analyze information, synthesize knowledge, and formulate informed judgments. By honing their critical thinking abilities, students are not only equipped to solve complex challenges but are also primed to navigate the intricate web of the modern world with acuity and astuteness.

2. Building Global Mindsets: In a world that is increasingly interconnected, the school recognizes the importance of imbuing students with a global perspective. Harapan Bangsa High School aspires to instill a profound understanding of diverse cultures, viewpoints, and global issues. This international outlook is nurtured through initiatives such as cross-cultural exchanges, international collaborations, and exposure to various worldviews. By embracing and respecting different perspectives, students are prepared to be compassionate and effective global citizens who contribute positively to the broader society.

3. Instilling Core Values for Success: The bedrock of Harapan Bangsa High School's mission lies in the cultivation of core values such as honesty, loyalty, perseverance, and compassion. These values are not only taught within the classroom but are also woven into the fabric of the school's environment. Through a harmonious interplay of mentorship, camaraderie, and ethical guidance, the school aims to develop individuals of integrity who not only excel academically but also exhibit qualities that enhance the collective welfare. These values empower students to become leaders who are not only proficient in their chosen fields but also conscious of their social responsibilities.
